During Covid

Draw your own Covid-19 negative sentiment trend line

Between mid February to the first week of March, we see a spike in negative sentiment in Covid related tweets. The negative sentiment goes down between mid march and mid April. Later in the summer months of May, June and July, we observe a significant spike in Negative sentiment. More recently, between mid August to the end of September the negative sentiment trend appears to be in a slightly downward trajectory. Additional data for October and November probably would have shown an upward trend in negative sentiment as covid related death and infection rates are increasing again.
